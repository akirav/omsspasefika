About Us

Pasefika Passion Pipeline (3P) is a collaborative effort between University of Hawaii Manoa-Office of Multicultural Students Services and Office of Vice President for University of Hawaii Community Colleges (UHCC). The main goal of this program is to increase the recruitment of non-Native Hawaiian Pacific Islanders (non-NHPI) in the State of Hawaii, to pursue learning at any UHCC. We also provide support for retention and transfer strategies once our students begin their college education at a UHCC.

College Admission Support

3P is available to support students in navigating the University of Hawaii admissions process. A 3P mentor will work one on one with students and their families in order to ensure they complete all the steps necessary for enrollment!

FAFSA Support

3P mentors are available to guide students through the complex financial aid application process known as the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).

Community Outreach

The 3P team outreaches to the community through various activities such as parent nights, college readiness workshops, and tutoring. If you are interested in having 3P share about their services with your community please contact us!
